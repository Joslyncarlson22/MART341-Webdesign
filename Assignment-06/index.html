Traditional Bruschetta

Ingredients

Bruchetta:

  • Two fresh tomatoes
  • 8-10 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 stick of fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 1 French Baguette
  • Balsamic reduction; for drizzle
  • 1 tablespoon Minced garlic
  • Olive Oil to taste

Directions

  1. OPTIONAL Preheat the oven to 375 0 F.

    2. How to Make Bruschetta

    1. Dice tomatoes and fresh basil leaves and set to side
    2. Slice mozzarella into thin pieces; about a half inch
    3. Slice baguette into quarter inch slices and place flat on a baking sheet or platter
    4. Place slices of mozzarella on top of each slice of baguette
    5. Mix diced tomatoes and fresh basil with a slight drizzle of olive oil and minced garlic
    6. Place mixture ontop of baguettes
    7. If served warm bake for 5 minutes at 375 if not proceed to next step
    8. Drizzle with balsamic reduction and serve!
