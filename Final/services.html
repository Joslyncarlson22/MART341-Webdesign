What makes a restaurant experience memorable? There is the foundation that is food. The culinary experience, whether the meal is enjoyed with family, friends or a good book, remains the fuel for life and celebration. For over 5000 years man has dabbled in fermentation for complimentary uses to that celebration. Advances in transportation and logistics have enabled us as never before, to enjoy the fruits of fermentation from all corners of the world, creating a daunting and undulating world of vintages, AVAs, DOCGs, fermenting techniques, oak programs, varietal distinctions and tipicity.
